Japan Desk @IITH

The Japan Desk is a sustainable platform for academic and industry-university collaboration between India and Japan. For this purpose, the Japan Desk provides one-stop services to support academic and industry-university collaboration both for IITH and Japanese academic institutions and industries.

Dean's Words :

As a part of the active collaboration between Japan and IIT Hyderabad, 'Japan Desk', is launched at IIT Hyderabad and this is the first-of-its-kind initiative among all IITs to facilitate India - Japan Academic & Industrial collaborations. We aim to provide a one-stop solution for all the student, faculty and company queries facilitated through this portal and unlock the full potential of our collaboration for mutual benefit. We anticipate enhanced Human Resources development in the fields of academia and industry through our strong collaboration.

About FRIENDSHIP 2.0

FRIENDSHIP 2.0 was launched in December 2021 as the second phase of the": Project for Future Researchers at IITH to Enhance Network Development with Scholarship of Japan in the Republic of India (FRIENDSHIP) Project," which was implemented by the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) since 2012 to promote industry-university collaboration between Japan and India FRIENDSHIP 2.0 aims to build a sustainable platform for academic and industrial collaboration between Japan and India at IITH, and to ensure that the human resources produced from this platform will continuously contribute to the academic and industrial development of both countries. We will also promote Japan-India collaboration from the perspective of Make in India and SDGs. As part of this effort, we have established the Japan Desk within IITH as a one-stop contact point for all information related to Japan-India collaboration, including study in Japan and joint research.

FRIENDSHIP 1.0

FRIENDSHIP Project aims to contribute to the further development in the educational and research activities of IITH through the world-class research network between IITH and higher education institutions & industrial clusters of Japan, whereby both can establish educational and research excellence in the field of science, technology and engineering in the future.
